Payment Methods

Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your reservation up to 24 hours before check-in for a full refund. Cancellations made within 24 hours of check-in will be charged for the first night.

Check-in and Check-out Times

Check-in is available from 3 PM. Check-out is at 11 AM. Late check-out can be arranged upon request and is subject to availability.

Long Stays

For stays longer than 30 days, please contact our reservations team for special rates and availability.

Amenities and Services

We offer complimentary breakfast, free Wi-Fi, a fitness center, and a swimming pool. Additional services include room service, laundry, and airport shuttle.

Pet Policy

Pets are welcome at our hotel. A pet fee of $50 per stay applies. Please inform us in advance if you are bringing a pet.
